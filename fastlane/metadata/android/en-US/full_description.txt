OpenTopo is an open-source Android GNSS survey app designed for Greek surveyors and engineers. It connects to any NMEA-compatible GNSS receiver via Bluetooth or USB-OTG, applies RTK corrections via NTRIP, and transforms WGS84/ETRS89 coordinates to GGRS87/EGSA87 (EPSG:2100) using the official HEPOS transformation model published by Ktimatologio S.A.

Features:
- Full HEPOS transformation (7-parameter Helmert + 2D correction grid) with sub-centimetre accuracy
- Bluetooth Classic and USB-OTG GNSS receiver support
- Built-in NTRIP v1/v2 client with presets for HEPOS, CivilPOS, and Hexagon SmartNet Greece
- Survey project and point management with configurable epoch averaging
- Stakeout navigation to target coordinates
- Map-centric interface with MapLibre
- Export to CSV, GeoJSON, and DXF

OpenTopo is free software licensed under AGPL-3.0. It does not collect any personal data or telemetry.

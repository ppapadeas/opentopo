OpenTopo Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

Overview

OpenTopo is an open-source GNSS surveying application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is handled.

Data We Collect

Location Data

OpenTopo uses precise GNSS location data from an external receiver connected via USB or Bluetooth. This data is:

  • Stored locally on your device in survey project databases
  • Never sent to our servers — we have no servers or cloud services
  • Exported only when you choose to share via CSV, GeoJSON, or DXF files

NTRIP Correction Data

When you connect to an NTRIP caster for RTK corrections, the app sends your approximate position (NMEA GGA sentence) to the caster you configure. This is required for the caster to provide correction data. The NTRIP connection is:

  • Initiated only by your explicit action
  • Sent to the caster address you configure (not our servers)
  • Used solely for receiving GNSS correction data

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No analytics or telemetry
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No personal information (name, email, phone)
  • No crash reporting to external services
  • No data is sent to OpenTopo developers

Local Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Survey projects and points — coordinates, accuracy, remarks, photos
  • Connection settings — NTRIP credentials, device preferences
  • App preferences — averaging time, baud rate, display format

All local data can be deleted by clearing app data or uninstalling the app.

Third-Party Services

The app loads map tiles from:

  • vathra.xyz — OpenStreetMap-based vector tiles (no tracking)
  • Ktimatologio (gis.ktimanet.gr) — Greek national orthophoto WMS (no tracking)
  • Protomaps CDN — Map font/glyph files (no tracking)

These services may log standard HTTP request data (IP address, user agent) per their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

OpenTopo is a professional surveying tool not directed at children under 13.
